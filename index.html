About Ali

Built on Trust.
Driven by People.

Real estate isn't where I started — it's where everything I am came together. I grew up in a family of four, moving eleven times across Iran, Switzerland, and five cities in the GTA. I know what it means to search for a home, not just a house.

My foundation was shaped in my family's jewelry business, where I learned early that craftsmanship, honesty, and putting people first aren't just values — they're good business. That grounding carried me through university, corporate sales, and eventually into real estate: the career where every lesson finally clicked.

Today, with 10+ years of experience and $100M+ in sales, I specialize in helping families in Vaughan who are ready for their next chapter. I'm not just your broker. I'm someone who has made that move eleven times and knows exactly how to make yours smooth.
