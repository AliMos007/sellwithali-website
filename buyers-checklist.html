Stage 04

Making an Offer

  • Confirm your pre-approval is current and in writing
  • Review comparable sales with your agent before offering
  • Decide on offer price, deposit, and closing date
  • Include appropriate conditions (inspection, financing)
  • Submit offer with certified deposit cheque ready
  • Be prepared to negotiate — know your walk-away number

Stage 07

Closing Day

  • Complete your final walk-through 24 hours before closing
  • Confirm all included items are present and working
  • Bring certified cheque or wire transfer for balance owing
  • Sign all closing documents with your lawyer
  • Receive keys and garage openers from seller's lawyer
  • Change all locks and set up utilities immediately
