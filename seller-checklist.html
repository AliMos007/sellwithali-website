Stage 02

Preparing Your Home

  • De-clutter all rooms — personal items, excess furniture
  • Deep clean every surface — hire a professional crew
  • Test all appliances, faucets, heating and cooling
  • Fresh neutral paint throughout — brighten every room
  • Curb appeal: mulch, fresh grass, plants at entry
  • Stage with neutral décor — let buyers see themselves here

Stage 07

Closing Day

  • Forward mail and notify all utilities one week prior
  • Confirm buyer's final walk-through is scheduled
  • Leave garage openers, gate codes, alarm codes
  • Leave appliance manuals and all repair invoices
  • Home fully vacated, clean, and ready for keys
  • Schedule closing Monday–Thursday for smoothest process
